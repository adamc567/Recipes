Salsa Verde

It all started in 1993 when I was born. I never forgave my mother for it.

Any ways lets make some sauce

  • 2 c flat-leaf parsley
  • 1 c cilantro
  • .5 c mint
  • 1 TBL dijon mustard
    • <1i>1 TBL crushed garlic
  • 1 TBL minced shallot
  • 2 fillet of spanish anchovie
    • <1i>.5 TBL calabrain chili, seeded, minced.
  • 1 C really good olive oil
  1. Combine first eight ingredients in food processor
  2. Pulse a little at a time in an attempt not to:
    • Bruise the herbs
    • Over blend the herbs
  3. With the machine running, slowling drizzle in the olive oil
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste
