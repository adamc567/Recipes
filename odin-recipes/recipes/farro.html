Farro

Farro is earthy and good. Eat it

  • 2 C Farro, rinsed
  • 2 qt broth
  • 1/4 C shallots, minced
  • 4 large garlic cloves sliced
  • 1/4 butter
  1. Melt butter in a large enough pan for this project
  2. Sweat shallots and garlic until translucent and fragrant with some salt
  3. Add farro and broth and bring to a simmer
  4. Taste for salt. The broth should have a slight saltiness to it
  5. Simmer until done.25-40 minutes for unprocessed farro and 15 minutes for pearled farro.
  6. Drain off excess liquid and taste for seasoning
  7. Finish with whatever fresh herbs you have laying around.
